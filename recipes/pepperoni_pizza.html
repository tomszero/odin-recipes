Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 prepared pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 20-30 slices pepperoni
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475 degrees F (245 degrees C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza peel or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  5. Arrange the pepperoni slices on top of the cheese.
  6. Drizzle the olive oil over the pizza and sprinkle with dried oregano. If you like a bit of heat, add crushed red pepper flakes as well.
  7. If using, sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the top for extra flavor.
  8. Transfer the pizza to the preheated oven (or onto the pizza stone if using) and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
